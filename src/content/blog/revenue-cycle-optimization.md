## Speed claims to payment

Identify common rejection triggers and create a standard review workflow before claims are submitted. That can reduce rework and accelerate reimbursements.

### Checklist for faster reimbursement

- Verify patient eligibility and benefits before the appointment.
- Confirm CPT and ICD-10 codes match the provider documentation.
- Review modifiers for bundled services and split claims correctly.
- Track trending denial reasons and update your front desk procedures.

## Build a data-driven billing cycle

Measure days in accounts receivable, denial rate, and clean claim rate. Reports help teams pinpoint where time is lost and which payers need follow-up.
