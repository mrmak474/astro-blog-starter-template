## Turn denied claims into recoveries

Every denied claim is an opportunity to strengthen your workflow. Track denials by payer and reason to build a targeted appeals strategy.

### Appeal with the right evidence

- Include the provider note and supporting diagnostics.
- Reference the payer's medical necessity policy.
- Submit corrected claims with the proper appeal rationale.
- Use escalation only when initial review does not resolve a valid claim.

## Reduce future denials

Create denial bundles for recurring errors, educate clinical staff on documentation gaps, and align coding reviews with the next pay cycle.
